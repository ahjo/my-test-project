Welcome to WEBinsta Mailing List Manager

  Welcome to Webinsta mailing list manager it was built to provide a centralized mailing list / newsletter system for small to medium website . Its simple yet quite powerfull . It support the standard subscribe and un-subscribe , remote verification , unsubscribe links , attachments and host of other such features . The best of all the total integration is less than 3 lines of code and can run with or without a database .

The various features are :

  • Very simple integration into existing design ("no index.php ").
  • Support for several database's .
  • Deletion, setting, editing and other options from a centralized control panel .
  • Suited for a small to medium website .
  • Good looking and fun to work with .
  • Multilingual support ( currently English , Dutch ) .
  • Templates for a real professional newsletter .
  • Connecivity to all major PHP driven scripts .

     

Introduction to WEBinsta Mailing List Manager

  Webinsta™ Mailing list/Newsletter manager provides a individual or a small business organisation to get a centralized mailing list/ newsletter system. It does not require on the users part to know any HTML or PHP . It tries to provide a simple but powerful means of centralized control over mailing lists and newsletters..

 It was built because we wanted to provide a simple, powerful, reliable mailing list system for the people who want these facilites but do not have the technical knowledge of handling the bigger and complex versions of such managers. With lots of options like subscribe and un-subscribe , remote verification , unsubscribe links , attachments , it provides the perfect system to control mailing lists and newsletters and provides you with an easy way to stay in touch with you clients .

 

System Requirements

  WEBinsta Mailing List Manager does not require any high end requirements but the following things are required .

  • PHP > 4.2 .
  • Database
  • A Mozilla or IE compaitable browser .
